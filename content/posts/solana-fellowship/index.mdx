Solana India Fellowship is a hands-on 8-week program to learn, earn and build on Solana. There is a new theme each week,
from NFTs to DeFi to Gaming, followed by quests, exercises, and Q&A sessions by experts. And it comes with a $2500
stipend!

## My journey with Solana

I came to know about Solana last year when I was learning rust. That's when I actually started diving deeper into the
crypto ecosystem and web3. I bought some SOL, created a wallet, started using dApps on Solana and read a lot.

Looking back, I was actually spoiled by Solana. I thought all blockchains are fast and have a cool wallet like
[Phantom](https://phantom.app/). This assumption was shattered pretty quickly when I started paying $80 gas fees on
ethereum, waiting for transactions to complete and couldn't use Phantom anymore. Solana was the only ecosystem I looked
forward to using.

## Getting into the fellowship

After all of this experimentation, I wanted to go full-time on web3 and see what possibilities lie there. Then I heard
about the hackathon on [SuperteamDAO's](https://twitter.com/SuperteamDAO) Twitter. Superteam is a collective which help
the most promising projects in the Solana ecosystem in the ascending world (India, South East Asia, Eastern Europe, and
Africa) launch and grow. I had been following Superteam for a while, and I particularly enjoyed their
[podcasts](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCi-pkXLbm7sqXFhV1NBLUfQ).

The [fellowship](https://solana-india-fellowship.devfolio.co/) is also an interesting program. An 8-week intense program
giving learning and earning opportunities and which also had amazing speakers and mentors. So I applied.

I had to fill a couple of application forms and gave an in-person interview. About a week back, I got an email from them
welcoming me to the fellowship! It was a surprisingly exciting moment for me. I couldn't wait to learn about this whole
new world of web3 and have this amazing team of mentors and supporters.
